As students we know that it is hard to plan your course, so we came up with an awesome idea on developing a tool to help you plan your course. Working in conjunction with eSolutions, this web app is guaranteed to make your life more easy.

We also would love to say thanks to everyone who have and are still supporting us by providing valuable feedback. Feel free to test out our app and submit feedback.

Start using our app

Our Goal

Our vision is for monPlan is a website that both new and current Monash students could use as a tool to help them plan their course, while not neccesarilly forcing students to abide but the rules. But also providing a central platform in which students can also access information. So that students can print off and share their plan with a their course advisor.

Features

Here's some quick features about our web app!

View Unit Info

View information about your unit, including the description, when and where the unit will be held, Preqs, Coreqs, how many credit points and how much the unit costs (based on 2017 costs and for Domestic CSP students). As well as SETU ratings for that unit.

Validation

With Preq, Coreq Validation Coming Soon, we are pleased to say Teaching Period validation is already in place.

Print, Share or Save your Course.

You can share and save your course as a link, otherwise you can also print it off and take it to a course advisor, easy right?.

Custom Units

In place of depreicated units, you can add 'Custom Units' to replace them. I mean what could go wrong?

Load A Course Map

You can start off with by loading a course or with the old way, picking your start and end years
