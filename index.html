About the Project

This project was first started by Eric Jiang (lorderikir), when it was discovered picking units that suited to his course was extremely difficult as you have to navigate through the student unit handbook. After consulting with the Monash community, it seemed that it was needed to design a course planner which was easy to use. So after, Josh Nelsson-Smith (Joshua-Xavier) and Saruabh Joshi also seemed extremly interested in this project and joined during the early stages of development.

We at monPlan, sought to develop a solution in which students can pick units across any faculty, any time during their course, which will inform them of the core units required for their degree, preqs and prohibitions for units and other suggestions.

We are also welcome to anyone to join us, we want a diverse team with different ideas and mindsets to bring monPlan to its potential.
